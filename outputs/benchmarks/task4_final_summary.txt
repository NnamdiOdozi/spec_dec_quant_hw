# Task 4 final summary: vLLM serving benchmark comparison

Benchmark setup:
- Model family: Qwen/Qwen3-8B
- Dataset: philschmid/mt-bench
- Number of prompts: 80
- Maximum request concurrency: 8
- Endpoint: /v1/completions
- Benchmark tool: vllm bench serve

| Configuration | Successful | Failed | Output tok/s | Request/s | Mean TTFT ms | Median TTFT ms | P99 TTFT ms | Mean TPOT ms | Mean ITL ms | Acceptance rate | Acceptance length |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Baseline | 80 | 0 | 1128.40 | 4.41 | 25.90 | 26.96 | 28.63 | 7.01 | 7.01 | n/a | n/a |
| EAGLE speculative | 80 | 0 | 1286.09 | 5.02 | 26.88 | 26.04 | 35.29 | 5.94 | 8.67 | 15.36% | 1.46 |
| FP8 | 80 | 0 | 1618.27 | 6.32 | 21.49 | 20.93 | 26.14 | 4.87 | 4.87 | n/a | n/a |
| FP8 + EAGLE speculative, best tuned run | 80 | 0 | 1284.03 | 5.02 | 67.81 | 24.97 | 464.93 | 5.70 | 8.25 | 22.45% | 1.45 |

Speedup versus baseline:
- EAGLE speculative: 1286.09 / 1128.40 = approximately 1.14x.
- FP8: 1618.27 / 1128.40 = approximately 1.43x.
- FP8 + EAGLE speculative: 1284.03 / 1128.40 = approximately 1.14x.

Rubric outcome:
- Speculative decoding exceeds 1250 tok/s: yes.
- FP8 exceeds 1550 tok/s: yes.
- FP8 + speculative exceeds 1750 tok/s: no.

Interpretation:
FP8 alone was the best-performing configuration in this run. EAGLE speculative decoding improved throughput on the base model, but the trained drafter had low acceptance beyond the first drafted token. Tuning FP8 + speculative to 2 draft tokens improved the default FP8 + speculative result, but it still did not outperform FP8 alone or reach the 1750 tok/s threshold.
